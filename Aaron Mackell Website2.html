SUPREMEEATZ ORIGIN


Est. Summer 2017

SupremeEatz: It's a LIFESTYLE, it's a CULTURE, a representation of you EATING in life and maximizing your fullest potential at the SUPREME level while the world SLEEPZ on you! SupremeEatz was founded at Hampton University by Aaron Mackell(Founder & Financial Manager), Tyler Mason(Co-Founder), Mason Parker(Co-Founder), and Douglas Martin II(Operations Manager). Four ingenious, intellectual, inspired, young black men who combined their passions for food with their ardent pursuit of financial literacy. Their services are offered through catering parties, cookouts, tailgates, special SupremeEvents and delivery(pre-orders accepted). Through their services they aim to inspire youth while they strive to pave a positive path for them! For more details follow us on instagram: @supremeeatz, facebook: @ supremeeatz, and twitter: @supremeeatz, be prepared to witness food at the SupremeLevel!

Mission Statement: SupremeEatz is dedicated to providing food services through a college based network. Our goal is to supply the best quality foods and quick service.
